Why Sommelier Cellars?

Liquidity Provider Management Tools

Sommelier Cellars are focused on Uniswap v3 which has high degree of capital efficiency in the Range Order structure.

Bi-Directional Ethereum Bridge

Ethereum transactions are managed by the most functional bridge optimized for extending Ethereum features for Liquidity Providers.

Data Driven Strategies

Sommelier validators leverage optimal liquidity management strategies based on blockchain transaction data and advanced price prediction models.

Non-Custodial Liquidity Oracle

Sommelier transactions are managed by the Sommelier blockchain validator set with decentralized governance. Sommelier itself does not control user funds.

Use Cases

Decentralized Portfolio Management

Validator set portfolio management with full user funds control.

Compounding Fees Across AMMs and Networks

Automatically compounding fees without need for active intervention.

Automated Portfolio Rebalancing

Automatically rebalance portfolio to focus on high yield Liquidity Provider opportunities and exit lower yielding opportunities.
